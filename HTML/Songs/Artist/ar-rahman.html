Artist

AR Rahman

A. R. Rahman is one of the most legendary and influential music composers in the global music industry. Renowned for his soulful compositions, groundbreaking fusion of genres, and spiritual depth, Rahman rose to international fame with his debut in Roja and went on to win Academy Awards, Grammy Awards, a BAFTA Award, a Golden Globe, and six National Film Awards. Blending Indian classical music with electronic beats, orchestral arrangements, and world sounds, his work has transformed the soundscape of Indian cinema. From timeless melodies to powerful anthems, Rahman's music transcends language and borders. His live performances are immersive and inspirational, earning him the title of "The Mozart of Madras" and a permanent place in the hearts of music lovers around the world.

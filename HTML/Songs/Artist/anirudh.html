Artist

Anirudh Ravichander

Anirudh Ravichander is one of the most dynamic and sought-after music composers in the Indian film industry. Known for his fresh sounds, chart-topping hits, and youthful energy, Anirudh shot to fame with his debut song "Why This Kolaveri Di" and has since composed music for numerous blockbuster Tamil and Telugu films. With a unique blend of electronic, classical, and folk influences, his music resonates deeply with audiences of all ages. Whether it's a peppy dance number or an emotional melody, Anirudh continues to push musical boundaries and redefine contemporary Indian cinema music.He has collaborated with top filmmakers and stars, delivering iconic albums that have left a lasting impact on South Indian music. His stage presence and live performances are electrifying, further cementing his status as a youth icon and musical trendsetter.

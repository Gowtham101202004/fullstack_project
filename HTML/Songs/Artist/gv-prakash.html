Artist

GV Prakash Kumar

GV Prakash Kumar is a multifaceted talent in the Indian film industry, widely celebrated as a composer, playback singer, and actor. Renowned for his melodious compositions and emotionally stirring soundtracks, GV Prakash made his mark with the film Veyil and quickly rose to fame with hits like Aayirathil Oruvan, Madrasapattinam, and Darling. His music seamlessly blends traditional South Indian melodies with modern orchestration, creating timeless tracks that appeal to both critics and audiences alike. Known for his versatility, GV Prakash has delivered everything from soulful romantic tunes to intense background scores that elevate cinematic storytelling. In addition to his music career, he has also established himself as a successful actor, showcasing his range and depth as a performer.
